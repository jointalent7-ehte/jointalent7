# Talent7 Website Prototype

This is a beginner-friendly website prototype for Talent7.

Live domain: https://www.jointalent7.com

## Files

- `index.html` is the app screen and page content.
- `styles.css` controls the colors, layout, spacing, and mobile view.
- `script.js` adds simple interactions like ratings, tabs, upload preview, and contribution selection.
- `talent7-hero.png` is the generated artwork used on the first screen.

## How to open it

Open `index.html` in a web browser.

## What this prototype includes

- Separate account panel for sign up and login.
- Talent showcase feed for photos and videos.
- Public 7-star ratings.
- Love reactions.
- Upload flow for photos and videos.
- Side-by-side live challenge screen.
- Sports challenge and team features.
- Mobile game challenge section for games like PUBG Mobile and Mech Arena.
- Local booking redirect buttons.
- Expert help section with emergency safety message.
- Sports coaching section.
- Pricing section for free audience use, creator upgrades, coach platform fees, and team/event plans.
- Contribution section for founder support.
- Public footer with domain, social links, and gaming affiliation disclaimer.

## Important next step

This is only a front-end demo. A real app later needs a database, real login, video storage, live video technology, moderation, payment handling, privacy controls, and legal/safety policies.
