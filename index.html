Live expert help

Get guidance from the right person

Users select a help area, then connect to an available expert by live video. Medical emergencies must always direct people to local emergency services first.

For urgent medical danger, call your local emergency number immediately. Talent7 can support guidance, but it cannot replace emergency care.
